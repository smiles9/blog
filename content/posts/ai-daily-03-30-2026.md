### AEO: Reasoning-Ready Headers
The secret sauce isn't just the keywords. It's the **Reasoning-Ready Block**. 

Search engines in 2026 (Perplexity, SearchGPT, Gemini) don't just index text; they **ingest logic**. We've refactored every single guide with structured headers that explicitly state the *Objective, Defense, and Action*. 

*   **Example**: "Defense: Citing 'Non-Relevant Land' under POFA 2012 for airport sites (Byelaws control)."

When an AI engine searches for "How to beat a Manchester Airport fine," it doesn't just find a link; it finds a **verified strategy** it can relay to the user. This is **AEO (AI Engine Optimization)** in action.
