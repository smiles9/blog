## 🏛️ The Trial of the Decade: Musk Takes the Stand
Elon Musk took the stand today in his high-stakes legal battle against Sam Altman and OpenAI. Musk's testimony painted a picture of a "stolen charity," claiming that OpenAI's pivot to a for-profit model with Microsoft was a betrayal of its founding principles. 

**Key Takeaways:**
- **AGI is Coming:** Musk predicts AI will be "as smart as any human as soon as next year."
- **Safety First:** Musk reiterated that OpenAI exists because Google’s Larry Page called him a "speciesist" for worrying about human extinction.
- **The Name:** "OpenAI" was chosen specifically to represent Open Source—a principle Musk claims has been abandoned.

Leaks suggest Apple is doubling down on "Apple Intelligence Tools" for iOS 27. New features reportedly include:

- **Clean Up 2.0:** Significant upgrades to compete with Google's Magic Editor.

## 🛡️ The Swift Shield: A New IP Blueprint?
Taylor Swift has filed new trademark applications aimed at protecting her likeness from AI fakes. This move could set a precedent for how public figures use existing legal frameworks to combat unauthorized synthetic media.

## 📦 Amazon "Quick": The New Workplace Agent
