# AI Daily Digest - April 25, 2026

## 1. The Era of the Billion-Dollar Solo Startup
As Anthropic’s CEO Dario Amodei famously predicted, we are witnessing the birth of the **Billion-Dollar Solopreneur.** This isn't just hyperbole; it's a mathematical outcome of the "Agentic Shift." When one human can manage a swarm of specialized agents—each handling the workload of a mid-sized department—the traditional concept of "scaling" via headcount becomes a legacy liability.

## 2. From SaaS to Agency as a Service
We are moving away from tools that users have to learn, toward agents that learn the user's business. In 2026, the most profitable solopreneurs aren't selling "software"—they are selling **Autonomous Outcomes.** Whether it's CV optimization or legal defense, the value lies in the *result*, not the interface. At Y Lab, we are building the blueprint for these "Outcome Engines."

## 3. Token ROI: The Only Metric That Matters
In the agent-manager economy, "Daily Active Users" is a vanity metric. The new North Star is **Token ROI.** How much revenue is generated for every 1M tokens of inference? By automating complex reasoning loops, we are seeing productivity multipliers that were technically impossible even 18 months ago.
