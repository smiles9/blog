# The Rise of Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

If you're still chasing "Blue Links," you're fighting a 2025 war in a 2026 world. 

As of late March 2026, **AI Overviews (SGE)** and **Answer Engines** (Perplexity, SearchGPT, Claude with Search) are capturing up to **65% of high-intent search traffic**. The traditional click-through model is evolving into a "Citation model." 

### The $+144\%$ ROI Pivot
Recent benchmarks show that businesses prioritizing **Citation Density** over raw keyword rankings are seeing a **+144% ROI** increase. Why? Because being the *source* of an AI-generated answer carries higher trust and conversion potential than just being "Result #1."

### How to Fix Your "AI Visibility Gap":
1.  **Schema-First Architecture**: AI bots don't "read" like humans; they ingest structured data. If your site lacks deep JSON-LD nesting, you're invisible to the crawlers that feed the Answer Engines.
2.  **Sentiment Authority**: Models now crawl platforms like Reddit, Quora, and niche forums to weigh your brand's authority. If nobody is talking about you there, the AI won't cite you as a trusted solution.
3.  **Stability Audits**: AI answers fluctuate. In 2026, "Stability" is a metric. If your brand is cited in only 20% of relevant AI Overviews, you have a stability gap that requires content reinforcement.

### Today's Actionable Insight:
Perform an "AI Visibility Audit." Ask Perplexity or Gemini a question your business solves. If your brand isn't in the citations, you are losing the 2026 traffic war. Stop tracking "rankings" and start tracking **"Citation Share."**

*Build the source. Capture the answer.*
