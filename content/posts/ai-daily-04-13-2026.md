### 🛠️ The Shift: From Chat to Agency
If your daily workflow involves copy-pasting text into a chat box, you are still operating in 2024. The winners in today's market are building **Agentic Stacks**. 

Instead of a single tool, they are using swarms:
- **Claude 4.5/5** for reasoning and complex planning.
- **Agentic frameworks** (like OpenClaw) for execution across files, web, and social.
- **Custom Knowledge Graphs** to provide the "memory" that static SaaS lacks.
